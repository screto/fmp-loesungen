Berechnen Sie die fehlenden Grössen:
a) $a = 33.4, b = 17.8, c = 24.7 \text{ cm}$
$\alpha = \arccos(\frac{b^2+c^2-a^2}{2bc}) \approx 102.13^\circ$
$\beta = \arcsin(\frac{b \cdot \sin(\alpha)}{a}) \approx 31.42^\circ$
$\gamma = 180^\circ - (\alpha+\beta) = 46.45^\circ$
$F = \frac{1}{2}bc \sin(\alpha) \approx 214.85 \text{ cm}^2$
b) $a = 45.65, b = 67.80, \gamma = 77.6^\circ$
$c = \sqrt{a^2+b^2-2ab \cos(\gamma)} \approx 73.18 \text{ cm}$
$\alpha = \arcsin(\frac{a \cdot \sin(\gamma)}{c}) \approx 37.56^\circ$
$\beta = 180^\circ - (\alpha+\gamma) = 64.84^\circ$
$F = \frac{1}{2}ab \sin(\gamma) \approx 1511.45 \text{ cm}^2$
