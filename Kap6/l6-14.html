Berechnen Sie die Winkel eines Dreiecks ABC mit:
a) $a = 3 \text{ cm}, b = 6 \text{ cm}, c = 4 \text{ cm}$
$\cos(\alpha) = \frac{b^2 + c^2 - a^2}{2bc} = \frac{6^2 + 4^2 - 3^2}{2 \cdot 6 \cdot 4} = \frac{43}{48} \approx 0.8958 \Rightarrow \alpha \approx 26.38^\circ$
$\cos(\beta) = \frac{a^2 + c^2 - b^2}{2ac} = \frac{3^2 + 4^2 - 6^2}{2 \cdot 3 \cdot 4} = \frac{-11}{24} \approx -0.4583 \Rightarrow \beta \approx 117.28^\circ$
$\gamma = 180^\circ - (\alpha + \beta) \approx 36.34^\circ$
b) $a = 50.8 \text{ m}, b = 53.6 \text{ m}, c = 39.4 \text{ m}$
$\cos(\alpha) = \frac{b^2 + c^2 - a^2}{2bc} = \frac{53.6^2 + 39.4^2 - 50.8^2}{2 \cdot 53.6 \cdot 39.4} \approx 0.4369 \Rightarrow \alpha \approx 64.09^\circ$
$\cos(\beta) = \frac{a^2 + c^2 - b^2}{2ac} = \frac{50.8^2 + 39.4^2 - 53.6^2}{2 \cdot 50.8 \cdot 39.4} \approx 0.3149 \Rightarrow \beta \approx 71.65^\circ$
$\gamma = 180^\circ - (\alpha + \beta) \approx 44.26^\circ$
