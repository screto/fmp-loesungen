Auftrag: Bestimmen Sie die fehlenden Seiten und Winkel des Dreiecks $ABC$:
a) $a = 5.5 \text{ m}, \alpha = 72^\circ, \beta = 56^\circ$
$\gamma = 180^\circ - (72^\circ + 56^\circ) = 52^\circ$
$b = \frac{a \cdot \sin(\beta)}{\sin(\alpha)} = \frac{5.5 \cdot \sin(56^\circ)}{\sin(72^\circ)} \approx 4.80 \text{ m}$
$c = \frac{a \cdot \sin(\gamma)}{\sin(\alpha)} = \frac{5.5 \cdot \sin(52^\circ)}{\sin(72^\circ)} \approx 4.56 \text{ m}$
b) $a = 6 \text{ m}, c = 5 \text{ m}, \alpha = 110^\circ$
$\sin(\gamma) = \frac{c \cdot \sin(\alpha)}{a} = \frac{5 \cdot \sin(110^\circ)}{6} \approx 0.7831$
$\gamma \approx 51.55^\circ$
$\beta = 180^\circ - (110^\circ + 51.55^\circ) = 18.45^\circ$
$b = \frac{a \cdot \sin(\beta)}{\sin(\alpha)} = \frac{6 \cdot \sin(18.45^\circ)}{\sin(110^\circ)} \approx 2.02 \text{ m}$
c) $a = 168.5 \text{ m}, b = 196 \text{ m}, \beta = 83^\circ$
$\sin(\alpha) = \frac{a \cdot \sin(\beta)}{b} = \frac{168.5 \cdot \sin(83^\circ)}{196} \approx 0.8533$
$\alpha \approx 58.57^\circ$
$\gamma = 180^\circ - (83^\circ + 58.57^\circ) = 38.43^\circ$
$c = \frac{b \cdot \sin(\gamma)}{\sin(\beta)} = \frac{196 \cdot \sin(38.43^\circ)}{\sin(83^\circ)} \approx 122.56 \text{ m}$
