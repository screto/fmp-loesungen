Berechnen Sie die fehlenden Seitenlängen und Winkel im gleichschenkligen Dreieck $ABC$ (Grundseite $c$, Schenkel $a=b$):
a) $b = 58.6 \text{ m} \quad ; \quad \alpha = 62.7^\circ$
$a = b = 58.6 \text{ m}$
$\beta = \alpha = 62.7^\circ$
$\gamma = 180^\circ - 2 \cdot 62.7^\circ = 54.6^\circ$
$c = 2 \cdot b \cdot \cos(\alpha) \approx 53.76 \text{ m}$
b) $a = 51.4 \text{ m} \quad ; \quad h_c = 43.8 \text{ m}$
$\sin(\alpha) = \frac{h_c}{a} = \frac{43.8}{51.4} \Rightarrow \alpha \approx 58.45^\circ$
$\beta = \alpha \approx 58.45^\circ$
$\gamma = 180^\circ - 2 \cdot \alpha \approx 63.10^\circ$
$c = 2 \cdot \sqrt{a^2 - h_c^2} \approx 53.94 \text{ m}$
c) $c = 86 \text{ m} \quad ; \quad h_a = 78 \text{ m}$
$\sin(\beta) = \frac{h_a}{c} = \frac{78}{86} \Rightarrow \beta \approx 65.09^\circ$
$\alpha = \beta \approx 65.09^\circ$
$\gamma = 180^\circ - 2 \cdot \beta \approx 49.82^\circ$
$a = b = \frac{c}{2 \cdot \cos(\beta)} \approx 102.08 \text{ m}$
d) $a = 4.8 \text{ m} \quad ; \quad h_b = 3.4 \text{ m}$
$\sin(\gamma) = \frac{h_b}{a} = \frac{3.4}{4.8} \Rightarrow \gamma \approx 45.10^\circ$
$\alpha = \beta = \frac{180^\circ - \gamma}{2} \approx 67.45^\circ$
$c = 2 \cdot a \cdot \cos(\alpha) \approx 3.68 \text{ m}$
