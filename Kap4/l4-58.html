Bestimmen Sie den Grenzwert der geometrischen Reihe \( S_n \).
a) \( 0.1 + 0.01 + 0.001 + \dots \)

\( a_1 = 0.1 \), \( q = 0.1 \)

$$ S = \frac{0.1}{1 - 0.1} = \frac{0.1}{0.9} = \frac{1}{9} $$
b) \( 0.35 + 0.0035 + 0.000035 + \dots \)

\( a_1 = 0.35 \), \( q = 0.01 \)

$$ S = \frac{0.35}{1 - 0.01} = \frac{0.35}{0.99} = \frac{35}{99} $$
c) \( 1 + \frac{1}{3} + (\frac{1}{3})^2 + \dots \)

\( a_1 = 1 \), \( q = \frac{1}{3} \)

$$ S = \frac{1}{1 - 1/3} = \frac{1}{2/3} = \frac{3}{2} = 1.5 $$
d) \( 2 + \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{8} + \frac{1}{32} + \dots \)

Prüfung Quotienten: \( \frac{1}{2} : 2 = \frac{1}{4} \); \( \frac{1}{8} : \frac{1}{2} = \frac{1}{4} \)

\( a_1 = 2 \), \( q = 0.25 \)

$$ S = \frac{2}{1 - 0.25} = \frac{2}{0.75} = \frac{2}{3/4} = \frac{8}{3} $$
e) \( \frac{5}{2} + \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{10} + \dots \)

Prüfung Quotienten: \( \frac{1}{2} : \frac{5}{2} = \frac{1}{5} \)

\( a_1 = 2.5 \), \( q = 0.2 \)

$$ S = \frac{2.5}{1 - 0.2} = \frac{2.5}{0.8} = \frac{25}{8} = 3.125 $$
f) \( 1 - \frac{1}{4} + (\frac{1}{4})^2 - \dots \)

Dies ist eine alternierende Reihe.

\( a_1 = 1 \), \( q = -\frac{1}{4} \)

$$ S = \frac{1}{1 - (-1/4)} = \frac{1}{1 + 1/4} = \frac{1}{5/4} = \frac{4}{5} = 0.8 $$
