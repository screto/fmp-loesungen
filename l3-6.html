Gegeben sind die drei Mengen:
$A = \{2; 5; 6; 7; 8; 15\}$
$B = \{1; 2; 6; 8\}$
$C = \{0; 6; 15; 17\}$

  • a) $A \cup B \cup C$ $A \cup B \cup C = \{0; 1; 2; 5; 6; 7; 8; 15; 17\}$
  • b) $(A \cap B) \cup C$ $(A \cap B) \cup C = \{0; 2; 6; 8; 15; 17\}$
  • c) $C \setminus A$ $C \setminus A = \{0; 17\}$
  • d) $A \cap B \cap C$ $A \cap B \cap C = \{6\}$
  • e) $(A \cap B) \cup (B \cap C)$ $(A \cap B) \cup (B \cap C) = \{2; 6; 8\}$
