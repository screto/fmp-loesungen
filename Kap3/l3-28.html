Aufgabe 3.28 - Potenzgesetze vereinfachen

Aufgabe 3.29 »
Aufgabe: Fassen Sie zusammen und vereinfachen Sie:
a) $a^7 \cdot a^{-6}$
$a^7 \cdot a^{-6} = a^{7+(-6)} = a^1 = a$
b) $x^p \cdot x^{-2}$
$x^p \cdot x^{-2} = x^{p+(-2)} = x^{p-2}$
c) $a^{(m-2)} \cdot a^{(3-m)}$
$a^{(m-2)} \cdot a^{(3-m)} = a^{(m-2)+(3-m)} = a^{m-2+3-m} = a^1 = a$
d) $\frac{x^2}{x^{-4}}$
$\frac{x^2}{x^{-4}} = x^2 \cdot x^4 = x^{2+4} = x^6$
e) $\frac{a^p}{a^{-3}}$
$\frac{a^p}{a^{-3}} = a^p \cdot a^3 = a^{p+3}$
f) $\frac{x^p}{x^{-q}}$
$\frac{x^p}{x^{-q}} = x^p \cdot x^q = x^{p+q}$
g) $4^{-3} \cdot 5^{-3}$
$4^{-3} \cdot 5^{-3} = (4 \cdot 5)^{-3} = 20^{-3} = \frac{1}{20^3} = \frac{1}{8000}$
h) $\left(\frac{9}{10}\right)^{-5} \cdot \left(\frac{5}{6}\right)^{-5}$
$\left(\frac{9}{10}\right)^{-5} \cdot \left(\frac{5}{6}\right)^{-5} = \left(\frac{9}{10} \cdot \frac{5}{6}\right)^{-5} = \left(\frac{45}{60}\right)^{-5} = \left(\frac{3}{4}\right)^{-5} = \left(\frac{4}{3}\right)^{5} = \frac{4^5}{3^5} = \frac{1024}{243}$
i) $a^{-6} \cdot b^{-6}$
$a^{-6} \cdot b^{-6} = (a \cdot b)^{-6} = (ab)^{-6} = \frac{1}{(ab)^6} = \frac{1}{a^6b^6}$
j) $(x+y)^{-4} \cdot (x-y)^{-4}$
$(x+y)^{-4} \cdot (x-y)^{-4} = [(x+y)(x-y)]^{-4} = (x^2-y^2)^{-4} = \frac{1}{(x^2-y^2)^4}$
k) $\frac{15^{-4}}{5^{-4}}$
$\frac{15^{-4}}{5^{-4}} = \left(\frac{15}{5}\right)^{-4} = 3^{-4} = \frac{1}{3^4} = \frac{1}{81}$
l) $\frac{x^{-p}}{y^{-p}}$
$\frac{x^{-p}}{y^{-p}} = \left(\frac{x}{y}\right)^{-p} = \left(\frac{y}{x}\right)^{p} = \frac{y^p}{x^p}$
m) $\frac{(a+b)^{-4}}{(a^2-b^2)^{-4}}$
$\frac{(a+b)^{-4}}{(a^2-b^2)^{-4}} = \left(\frac{a+b}{a^2-b^2}\right)^{-4} = \left(\frac{a+b}{(a+b)(a-b)}\right)^{-4} = \left(\frac{1}{a-b}\right)^{-4} = (a-b)^4$
n) $\frac{(x+5)^{-5}}{(x^2+5x)^{-5}}$
$\frac{(x+5)^{-5}}{(x^2+5x)^{-5}} = \left(\frac{x+5}{x^2+5x}\right)^{-5} = \left(\frac{x+5}{x(x+5)}\right)^{-5} = \left(\frac{1}{x}\right)^{-5} = x^5$
o) $10^3 : 5^3$
$10^3 : 5^3 = \frac{10^3}{5^3} = \left(\frac{10}{5}\right)^3 = 2^3 = 8$
p) $\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^{-2} : \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^2$
$\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^{-2} : \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^2 = \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^{-2-2} = \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^{-4} = 3^4 = 81$
q) $\left(\frac{x}{2}\right)^{3} \cdot \left(\frac{x}{4}\right)^{-3}$
$\left(\frac{x}{2}\right)^{3} \cdot \left(\frac{x}{4}\right)^{-3} = \left(\frac{x}{2}\right)^{3} \cdot \left(\frac{4}{x}\right)^{3} = \left(\frac{x}{2} \cdot \frac{4}{x}\right)^{3} = \left(\frac{4x}{2x}\right)^{3} = 2^3 = 8$
r) $\left(\frac{a}{4}\right)^{-1} : \left(\frac{a}{6}\right)^2$
$\left(\frac{a}{4}\right)^{-1} : \left(\frac{a}{6}\right)^2 = \left(\frac{4}{a}\right) : \left(\frac{a^2}{36}\right) = \frac{4}{a} \cdot \frac{36}{a^2} = \frac{144}{a^3}$
