Vereinfachen Sie:

  1. a)$2^6 \cdot 5^6 =\ ?$ $2^6 \cdot 5^6 = (2 \cdot 5)^6 = 10^6 = 1\,000\,000$
  2. b)$8^3 \cdot 125^3 =\ ?$ $8^3 \cdot 125^3 = (8 \cdot 125)^3 = 1000^3 = 1\,000\,000\,000$
  3. c)$6^4 \cdot 0.5^4 =\ ?$ $6^4 \cdot 0.5^4 = (6 \cdot 0.5)^4 = 3^4 = 81$
  4. d)$3.5^2 \cdot 0.2^2 =\ ?$ $3.5^2 \cdot 0.2^2 = (3.5 \cdot 0.2)^2 = 0.7^2 = 0.49$
  5. e)$\left(\frac{1}{4}\right)^5 \cdot 8^5 =\ ?$ $\left(\frac{1}{4}\right)^5 \cdot 8^5 = \left(\frac{1}{4} \cdot 8\right)^5 = 2^5 = 32$
  6. f)$\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^3 \cdot \left(\frac{4}{5}\right)^3 =\ ?$ $\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^3 \cdot \left(\frac{4}{5}\right)^3 = \left(\frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{4}{5}\right)^3 = \left(\frac{4}{10}\right)^3 = \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^3 = \frac{8}{125}$
  7. g)$2^4 \cdot 3^4 \cdot 4^4 =\ ?$ $2^4 \cdot 3^4 \cdot 4^4 = (2 \cdot 3 \cdot 4)^4 = 24^4$
  8. h)$3^5 \cdot \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^5 \cdot \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^5 =\ ?$ $3^5 \cdot \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^5 \cdot \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^5 = \left(3 \cdot \frac{2}{3} \cdot \frac{1}{3}\right)^5 = \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^5$
  9. i)$3^n \cdot 4^n =\ ?$ $3^n \cdot 4^n = (3 \cdot 4)^n = 12^n$
  10. j)$8^{(2x+1)} \cdot 125^{(2x+1)} =\ ?$ $8^{(2x+1)} \cdot 125^{(2x+1)} = (8 \cdot 125)^{(2x+1)} = 1000^{(2x+1)}$
  11. k)$15^{(p-1)} \cdot \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{(p-1)} =\ ?$ $15^{(p-1)} \cdot \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{(p-1)} = \left(15 \cdot \frac{2}{3}\right)^{(p-1)} = 10^{(p-1)}$
  12. l)$(-2.5)^n \cdot (-0.6)^n =\ ?$ $(-2.5)^n \cdot (-0.6)^n = (-2.5 \cdot -0.6)^n = 1.5^n$
  13. m)$x^6 \cdot y^6 =\ ?$ $x^6 \cdot y^6 = (x \cdot y)^6 = (xy)^6$
  14. n)$(a+b)^4 \cdot (a-b)^4 =\ ?$ $(a+b)^4 \cdot (a-b)^4 = ((a+b)(a-b))^4 = (a^2-b^2)^4$
  15. o)$(-x)^{(2p)} \cdot y^{(2p)} =\ ?$ $(-x)^{(2p)} \cdot y^{(2p)} = ((-x)y)^{(2p)} = (-xy)^{(2p)}$
  16. p)$3^n \cdot \left(\frac{x}{3}\right)^n =\ ?$ $3^n \cdot \left(\frac{x}{3}\right)^n = \left(3 \cdot \frac{x}{3}\right)^n = x^n$
  17. q)$\left(\frac{x}{y}\right)^m \cdot \left(\frac{y}{x}\right)^m =\ ?$ $\left(\frac{x}{y}\right)^m \cdot \left(\frac{y}{x}\right)^m = \left(\frac{x}{y} \cdot \frac{y}{x}\right)^m = 1^m = 1$
  18. r)$\left(\frac{3a}{4}\right)^m \cdot \left(\frac{2}{9a}\right)^m =\ ?$ $\left(\frac{3a}{4}\right)^m \cdot \left(\frac{2}{9a}\right)^m = \left(\frac{3a}{4} \cdot \frac{2}{9a}\right)^m = \left(\frac{2}{6}\right)^m = \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^m$
